## Performance comparisons and key learnings

- The perf runner measures average elapsed times across repeated iterations and verifies sorting correctness by comparing results to `Array.Sort`.
- Key learnings:
  - For random data, `Array.Sort` is highly optimized and often matches or outperforms a simple QuickSort implementation.
  - Microbenchmarks should warm up the JIT, repeat runs, and compare outputs for correctness.
  - Reflection invocation adds overhead; prefer direct calls when measuring algorithm-only performance.
